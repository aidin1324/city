Live your Parisian dream, and create colorful, buttery croissants

Step into the world of Netflix’s Emily in Paris with an exclusive macaron-making experience in the very workshop where the original cast created their own sweet treats. Under the guidance of a Parisian pastry chef, you’ll mix, pipe, and decorate your own colorful macarons, choosing from an array of flavors and playful designs inspired by Emily’s adventures in the City of Light.

As your creations bake, enjoy fun series-inspired conversation cards, and immerse yourself in the show’s charm with an Emily in Paris playlist setting the perfect ambiance. Add a touch of magic by painting your macarons with colorful edible dust using brand stencils, making each one as elegant and charming as Paris itself.

To complete the experience, take home your handmade macarons in a box, along with exclusive Emily in Paris souvenirs. A hands-on, chic, and delicious way to taste the magic of Paris - because nothing says 'je ne sais quoi' like a perfectly-crafted macaron.

FAQ

Do you run private Emily in Paris experiences?

Yes, our team of experience experts can craft the perfect Emily in Paris private experience for you. Get in touch with us at bonjour@parisbyemily.com to bring your idea to life.

Where is the meeting point?

Le 80 by Lili & Clo: 80 rue de Cléry, 75002 Paris. Look for the red storefront with “LE 80” written in white.

Nearest metro: Sentier (Line 3) – 3 min walk.

Please arrive a few minutes early and be ready for the experience to start right on time. Late arrivals cannot be accepted.

The door may open at the exact starting time, or just a few minutes before, thank you for your patience.

Are the Emily in Paris experiences wheelchair accessible?

We strive to make our experiences enjoyable for everyone, but please note that most of our activities are not suitable for individuals with mobility impairments or wheelchair users.

  • Walking Tours and Food Tours: These experiences involve significant walking and uneven terrain, making them unsuitable for wheelchair users or those that have mobility impairments.
  • Boat Cruise: Unfortunately, this is not wheelchair accessible.

If you have specific accessibility needs, we recommend reaching out to us at bonjour@parisbyemily.com before booking, and we’ll do our best to assist or suggest suitable alternatives.

Are pets allowed on the tours?

Pets are not permitted on experiences; however, service animals are welcome at the owner’s responsibility.
